Our Purpose

the most potent greenhouses gases

Global climate change is one of the most important and pressing challenges faced by our planet, and refrigerants are some of the most potent greenhouse gases ever created. Older refrigerants can have global warming potentials (GWP) more than ten thousand times greater than an equivalent mass of carbon dioxide.

We’re committed to fighting climate change by hunting down and safely destroying these refrigerants before they are vented to the atmosphere.

Our Process

hunt collect destroy

hunt

We buy eligible refrigerant gases from individuals, businesses and municipalities, instead of charging fees for handling and disposal. This provides an incentive for owners of old refrigerant stock to properly dispose of their material.

collect

We transfer the refrigerant from aging containers into larger and more secure holding tanks, to avoid venting.

destroy

We incinerate the refrigerant at an approved facility, certified to destroy at least 99.99% of the material to This generates carbon offset credits, which fund our ongoing work.

Our Goal

one million tonnes CO2e

In order to avoid the worst effects of climate change, the world must drawdown to 50% of current CO2 emmisions by 2030, and zero CO2 emmisions by 2050.

Refrigerant management and destructon has been identified by Project Drawdown as one of the most effective and important strategies to achieve these goals.

Our goal at Refrigerant Hunters is to mitigate the CO2 equivalent (CO2e) of 1 million tonnes of CO2.
